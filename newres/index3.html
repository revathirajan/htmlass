Welcome to Mugs

Mugs,situated at Pondicherry is a premium leisure resort beautifully lounged at an estuary between Chunnambar River and the Bay of Bengal.

Mugs celebrates Pondicherry in all its true sense- the spirit, the architecture and the revelry.

Pondicherry is one of the most popular tourist destinations in South India. It is also known as The French Riviera of the East.
